Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about UFC Georgia

UFC Georgia is an unofficial fan community dedicated to celebrating Georgian mixed martial artists competing in the Ultimate Fighting Championship. We provide news, event coverage, fighter profiles, and a platform for fans across Georgia and the diaspora to connect and share their passion for MMA.

No, UFC Georgia is an independent fan community and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to UFC, Endeavor Group, or any official UFC entity. We are passionate fans who create content to support and promote Georgian fighters on the global stage.

We organize regular watch parties at partner venues across Tbilisi for major UFC events. Registered members receive email notifications about upcoming watch parties. Follow our social media channels or register on our website to stay updated on the next event gathering.

Georgia currently has multiple fighters on the UFC roster, including Bantamweight Champion Merab Dvalishvili, Featherweight Champion Ilia Topuria, featherweight striker Giga Chikadze, and middleweight Roman Dolidze. Visit our Home page for detailed fighter profiles and statistics.

Registration is free and takes less than two minutes. Visit our Register page, fill out the form with your details, select your favorite fighter, and create an account. You must be at least 18 years old to register. Once registered, you'll receive event notifications and community updates.
