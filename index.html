About UFC & MMA

The Ultimate Fighting Championship is the world's premier mixed martial arts organization

The Ultimate Fighting Championship (UFC) is the largest and most prestigious mixed martial arts (MMA) promotion in the world. Founded in 1993, the UFC has grown from a single event into a global sports phenomenon broadcast in over 170 countries and territories, reaching hundreds of millions of fans worldwide.

Mixed Martial Arts (MMA) is a full-contact combat sport that combines techniques from boxing, wrestling, Brazilian jiu-jitsu, Muay Thai, judo, karate, and other disciplines. Fighters compete inside the iconic eight-sided Octagon, testing their skills across striking, grappling, and ground fighting.

Georgia has emerged as a powerhouse nation in the UFC, producing multiple champions and top-ranked contenders. With a rich wrestling tradition dating back centuries — including the ancient Georgian sport of Khridoli — Georgian athletes bring unparalleled grappling expertise and fighting spirit to the global stage.

From the mountains of Svaneti to the streets of Tbilisi, Georgian fighters carry the pride of their nation into every bout, inspiring a new generation of martial artists across the Caucasus region.
